Full Senate Votes and Data

Here's a searchable table of U.S. Senators and analyses of their voting patterns during the 2019 calendar year, scraped from the US Senate website. Party loyalty is measured out of 100 as a percentage of how often a party member votes in agreement with the party leader; all columns to the after the party loyalty column display party loyalty in votes regarding specific categories. Independent Senators were calculated as having -100% loyalty.
